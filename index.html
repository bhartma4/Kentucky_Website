  • Kentucky is known for its rich culture, horse racing, and bourbon distilleries.
  • About 95% of the world’s bourbon is produced in Kentucky.
  • The Kentucky Derby, held annually in Louisville, is the oldest continuous horse race in the U.S.
  • Kentucky is the birthplace of KFC and its famous founder, Colonel Harland Sanders.
  • Milk is the official state drink, but Kentucky is better known for its bourbon.
  • The 16th U.S. president, Abraham Lincoln, was born in a log cabin in Hodgenville, Kentucky.
  • Kentucky’s state flower is the goldenrod.
